--------------------------------- About me

Extra stuff

After deciding not to join the marines at age 16 and instead pursuing theatre, I had plenty of expereince in environmets that at the least would be deemed non-inclusive. Upon reflection the time spent in these environments as well as creative ones since has meant that I have insight into how various institutions function and an awareness of people and how their brains can be affected by them.

Cont.

Furthermore, growing up with ADHD meant that I struggled to see myself functioning to the best of my abilities and found it difficult to find healthy outlets, theatre allowed me to explore how my brain works and engineer a way of working that was compatible for both me and the "neurotypical" world. As well as this, forum theatre introduced me to methods of working with people in various contexts around issues such as inclusion and well-being that I plan on building on throughout my career.
